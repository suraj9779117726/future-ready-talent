About The Conference

A conference call is an audio call in which multiple participants all join the same call at the same time. Most commonly, people join conference calls by dialing a shared conference number on their phones.

conference call has also been used to describe group calls held by computer using software like Skype, UberConference, and other computer-based audio technologies.

A conference call that includes real-time video of participants is called a video conference, and one that includes screen sharing or other real-time content sharing is known as a web conference.

FAQ

Do I need to prepare about anything before the tests?

Not really. Just have a good night’s sleep. Eat a good breakfast. Relax. Have a fresh state of mind. These tests don’t need you to have any prior preparation as they are not based on syllabus or subjects.

Can parent/s accompany the child when the tests occur? / Are parents required for the testing?

Parents are not really required during the testing. Parent/s are required to be there for the counselling sessions. However, if the student is of 18 years of age and above, they could chose to take a session with or without their parents.

Can I split the tests between different days because I am busy?

Yes. You could do that. You need to inform us at the time of your appointment, and schedule appointment dates accordingly.

Does the student need to carry any academic or extra-curricular certificates?

No. That would not be needed.

How will the workshop on “Focused Personality Booster” help?

The main outline of the workshop is focused boost on “Communication Skills” and “Self-Esteem” issues. The workshop objective is to identify the concern areas of the participants and tailor-make the contents of the workshop in accordance to the communication and self-esteem concerns and issues of each participants. Bringing greater effectiveness.
